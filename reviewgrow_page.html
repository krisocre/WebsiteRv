How Many Reviews Does A New Business Need?

The ideal number of reviews for a new business depends on your industry, local competition, and business goals, but general benchmarks can guide your strategy.

Most customers become comfortable engaging with businesses that have at least 10-15 reviews. This threshold signals basic credibility and suggests enough customer experience to form a reliable opinion. Below this number, many potential customers will hesitate or choose competitors instead.

For competitive industries like restaurants, salons, medical practices, or home services, you'll need 20-30 reviews to stand out.
